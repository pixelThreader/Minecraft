### Commands in this mod

- `/function max_ench:get_max_iron` — Gives complete max-enchanted Iron armor, tools, and food (64 porkchops + 64 enchanted golden apples + 64 golden apples)
- `/function max_ench:get_max_diamond` — Gives complete max-enchanted Diamond armor, tools, and food
- `/function max_ench:get_max_netherite` — Gives complete max-enchanted Netherite armor, tools, and food
